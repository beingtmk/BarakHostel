Barak, the 7th hostel in the IITG fraternity, derives it's name from the River Barak. It is a major river in north-eastern India and is a part of the Surma-Meghna River System. It rises in the Manipur hills and enters the plains near Lakhipur. The hostel brings a cheerful environment to all its boarders. In its very first year, the hostel created history by securing third place in inter hostel sports meet. Thus it showed its muscle power in sports and announced itself as the future hub of sports champions. Boarders of the hostel, as they call themselves, barakites are all set to show their spirit. "Come on guys. Its show time. Show the spirit, the everlasting spirit. Because Champs are here."
