Hostel Mess

From the rugged terrain of the west to the occeanic adventure of the east and from the chillness of the north to the serene land of the south, the fooding experience of the barak mess takes you through all these food cultures. The mess offers a huge variety of food items from acoss all parts of the country. To ensure the satisfaction of students from all the different parts of the country, the hostelites elect a mess convener who in turn appoints a committee under him. The committee consists of students from all parts of india. They inspect the food quality and advice the convener accordingly. From this year onwards, iitg has started the mess coupon and subscription system. The Barak hostel also has a canteen and a juice centre which cater to the vast appetites of the hostelites. Maximum Capacity: 504 Vacancies:35
